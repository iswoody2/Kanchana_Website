About Me

Kanchana Kumari is a self-taught makeup artist based between Berkshire and West London, with a passion for beauty, fashion, and self-expression. Known for her flawless technique and eye for detail, Kanchana blends classic glamour with modern trends to create looks that empower and inspire. Whether it's editorial shoots, special occasions, or everyday beauty, she brings creativity, confidence, and a touch of artistry to every face she works on.
